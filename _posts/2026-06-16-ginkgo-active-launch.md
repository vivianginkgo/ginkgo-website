We are beyond excited to finally pull back the curtain on something we’ve been working on for a long time: the official launch of **Ginkgo Active**.

If you've ever felt like most fitness apps were built for people who are *already* athletes, you aren't alone. The app market is flooded with tracking tools and intense, pre-packaged workouts. But what about the rest of us? What about individuals who want to safely navigate joint pain, chronic conditions, or simply age with strength and independence?

That is exactly why we built Ginkgo Active. We aren't just giving you a workout tracker; we are giving you a complete, AI-powered Health GPS.

### **It Takes Two Engines to Fly**

As our Founder and CEO, Maggie Deng, puts it: *"When we set out to develop our exercise prescription software, we recognized that it's like a two-engine plane—one engine being the exercise plan itself, and the other being the behavior support plan."*

Getting healthy isn't just about knowing *what* to do; it's about staying motivated to do it safely, day after day. To solve this, we stepped away from boring, static workout videos and built Ginkgo Active on a stunning 3D game engine.

When you join, you don't just get a list of exercises. You get:

* **Professor Ginkgo:** Your AI-powered health expert who assesses your unique needs and adapts your plan in real-time.  
* **A Private Virtual Island:** A beautiful, customizable landscape (think cherry blossoms or sunny beaches) where your "digital twin" guides you through your daily movements.  
* **Holistic Support:** Daily guidance that looks at your physical capabilities, mental well-being, and sleep to ensure every session is entirely risk-free.

### **Real Lives, Changed**

We’ve been testing Ginkgo Active with early adopters, and the results have been nothing short of incredible. We've seen users like Ping, a 53-year-old recovering from hip replacement surgery, confidently reclaim her mobility and hike through Europe. We've seen multi-generational families train safely in their living rooms, waving goodbye to issues like night-time leg cramps.

This isn't just about exercise. It's about preventing decline before it starts, and making elite-level longevity coaching accessible to anyone with an iPad or smartphone.
